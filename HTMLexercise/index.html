Martha Stewart's Grilled Cheese Recipe

A Cheese sandwich is a basic sandwich made generally with one or more varities of cheese on any sort of bread, such as flat bread or whole wheat bread. In addition to the cheese, it may also include spreads such as butter or mayonnaise. Try this recipe for a delicious treat. For More information on grilled cheese sand-wiches consult the Wikipedia article.

Ingredients Needed

  • 2 Slices of bread
  • 4 Slabs of American Cheese
  • 3 Tablespoons of butter

Directions

  1. Pre-heat skillet
  2. Generously slather 3 table-spoons of butter onto bread slices
  3. Place bread on skillet and cheese slices on top of bread
  4. Wait for cheese to melt
  5. Place pieces of bread together and enjoy! Nom nom.
